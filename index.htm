About Us:

Elite is a Discussion group of Twelve(12) Students at Cavendish University Uganda at the fucalty of Science and Technology at Siyani Campus. It was founded in August 2024 by a group of Visionary Students.

Core Values:

1. Inclusivity: Embracing diversity, welcoming diverse perspectives. 2. Respect: Valuing individual opinions, fostering open-minded dialogue.
